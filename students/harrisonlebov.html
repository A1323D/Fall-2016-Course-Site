What is an Affidavit of Indigency?

An "Affidavit of Indigency" is a court form that a person can file in the clerk's office if they cannot afford to pay normal court fees and costs. If the court agrees that you should not have to pay your court fees, you may be able to have part or all of your court fees waived*. 

*Waiver means that you would not have to pay these fees and costs

To determine if you qualify to have part or all of your normal court fees waived, please answer the questions below.

If you have determined that you qualify to have your normal court fees waived, please click the button below to the right for a copy of the Affidavit of Indigency court form. If you would like help filling out the Affidavit of Indigency court form, please click the button below to the left.

*** After completing the Affidavit of Indigency, you will still need to file it with the clerk's office of your local courthouse to get your court fees waived ***
