What is an Affidavit of Indigency?

An Affidavit of Indigency is a court form that a person can file in the clerk's office, which asserts that you cannot afford to pay normal court fees and costs associted with litigation. If the court deems that you are "indigent," you may be able to have part or all of your normal court fees waived*. 

*Waiver means that you would not have to pay these fees and costs

To determine if you are indigent, and thus entitled to have part or all of your normal court fees waived, please take the guided interview below.
