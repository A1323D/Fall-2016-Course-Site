What exactly is a nonprofit?

A nonprofit is commonly referred to as a "501(c) organization," a "non-business entity," or a "charitable organization." 

A nonprofit organization is an organization with a purpose of which is something other than making a profit. 

A nonprofit organization is often dedicated to furthering a particular social cause or advocating for a particular point of view.

What does the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) have to say about nonprofits?

The major benefit of becoming a charitable organization is they are tax-exempt under Section 501(c)(3) of the tax code so long asthe organization is operated and organized exclusively for an exempt purpose set forth in 501(c)(3).

What are your options when applying for nonprofit status? 

1. Form 1023: The generic nonprofit filing form. 
2. Form 1023-EZ: A simplified version of Form 1023.  

What exactly is Form 1023-EZ and why was it enacted? 

On July 1, 2014, the Internal Revenue Service introduced a new, shorter application form to help small charities apply for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status more easily.

“This is a common-sense approach that will help reduce lengthy processing delays for small tax-exempt groups and ultimately larger organizations as well,” said IRS Commissioner John Koskinen. “The change cuts paperwork for these charitable groups and speeds application processing so they can focus on their important work."

The new Form 1023-EZ, available today on IRS.gov, is three pages long, compared with the standard 26-page Form 1023. Most small organizations, including as many as 70 percent of all applicants, qualify to use the new streamlined form.

The change will allow the IRS to speed the approval process for smaller groups and free up resources to review applications from larger, more complex organizations while reducing the application backlog.

Are you eligible to use Form 1023-EZ for 501(c)(3) Streamlined Application? 

In general, organizations whose revenue is less than $50,000 and whose assets are less than $250,000 can use Form 1023-EZ for the 501(c)(3) Streamlined Application.
